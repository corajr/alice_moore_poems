\PoemTitle{Equinox}
\label{ch:equinox}
\settowidth{\versewidth}{There is a border of pale }
\begin{verse}[\versewidth]
Winter is dark\\*
It is a monk's cell,\\*
A long night---\\*
But at dawn \\*
There is a border of pale \\*
Fire, green, \\*
Then greener \\*
Still. Soon enough\\*
---just soon enough---\\*
It will ignite \\*
\& the snows \\*
Melt \& the black \\*
Season lighten.
\end{verse}
