\begin{verse}[\versewidth]
               ---The bus moves too\\
Quickly\\
For you to see\\
The naked

Woman\\
Framed in her kitchen\\
Window, laughing---\\
Laughing, her

Body---resilient\\
Forked\\
Sapling---ah!\\
Her deciduous body

Cleft by laughter\\
Gone---
\end{verse}
