\PoemTitle[Albany: But when a man / Grins like a wolf]{}\label{ch:lear_av}
\settowidth{\versewidth}{White-green and phosphorescent---}
\begin{verse}[\versewidth]
\Character{Albany} \hspace*{1\vgap} But when a man\\*
Grins like a wolf,\\*
Or a woman's eye gleams---

White-green and phosphorescent---\\*
When wolfish cries echo and rise\\*
Out of their hearts, then

The wolves have shed\\*
Their natural skins\\*
And find themselves monstrous

In ours.
\end{verse}
